Wanna play with a baby lemur?


Please consider helping us raise money to add a baby brown lemur into the family at Exotic Experience!


Spring is here and we want to add another lemur to our menagerie here at Exotic Experience. You can see how they animals love each other and have a wonderful life! Our guests are able to come in and play with lemurs, kangaroos, deer, and a kinkajou. Through the Animal Fund at gofundme.com we will be able to give a baby lemur the opportunity to live in a fantastic environment and help educate people about the species! Thank you in advance for any help you can give.
