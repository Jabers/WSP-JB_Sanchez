WHAT IS "EXOTIC EXPERIENCE?"

Come meet the stars of the You Tube video sensation "Kangaroo and Lemur Play Tag!"

We also have Jeffery, the mini donkey, Sam the mini horse, some bunnies, tortoises and Gypsy Horses!


Scroll down to read the "Quick Facts" to find out just how rare these animals are and how special and amazing it is for you to be able to experience them.



DOZER

Dozer is a nine-year-old Lab/Beagle cross that loves EVERYONE! He loves to be touched by ANYONE and ANYTHING! He loves to play with our exotic animals!


K.C.

K.C. is an eight-year-old tortoise-shell female cat. She LOVES kangaroo joeys and babysits them when they are in their pouches!


KEEVA

Keeva is a three-month-old Irish Wolfhound puppy--she is all puppy right now...and BIG!


LOLLI

Lolli is a two-year-old tri-colored female ruffed lemur. She is super sweet and gentle--loves to play and have her belly and armpits scratched. NOTE TO GUESTS: Ruffed lemurs are critically endangered; and a black and white crossed with a red ruffed would not happen in the wild, making Lolli one of a kind and very unique. Look for babies in the future!


LOVE BUG

Love Bug, a.k.a. "Bug," is a one-year-old male Western gray kangaroo who will play with anyone and anything put in front of him. He is a stickler on grooming and believes all the animals need a bit of that! He is a sweetheart and loves to hug people and other animals.


LIAM

Liam is a three-year-old male ring-tailed lemur, a.k.a. "Boss." He is my personal pet and sleeps in the house with me, though he feels it is his job to protect not only me, but the other animals, as well. He loves them all and does NOT take kindly to other humans coming in to pet ANYONE! Unfortunately, he will be caged during guest visits, though he is fun to watch in the videos.
