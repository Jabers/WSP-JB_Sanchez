Site Intent

Define the goals for both the client company and the consumer/end-user for this site.

    Client Goals:
  • To get visitors to "The most unique interaction with exotic animals in Orlando!!!"
  • To highlight the animals that can be experienced hands-on
  • To inform the visitor of the many ways to interact with the animals
  • To get the visitor to view their youtube videos
  • To raise funds for purchasing another exotic animal to add to their collection
  • To sell t-shirts to help fund the experience
    End-user Goals:
  • discover what makes the exotic experience exotic
  • schedule a visit for a hands-on experience with atypical zoo animals
